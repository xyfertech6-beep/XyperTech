Our story

A small team that treats your brand like our own.

Xyfer Tech started with a simple frustration: businesses in Nepal were hiring three different freelancers for a website, a logo and an Instagram page — and getting three different brands. We build all of it as one.

Why we exist

One connected team, instead of three disconnected freelancers.

We founded Xyfer Tech in Kathmandu to give growing businesses a single, accountable team for their entire digital presence — a website that matches your brand, and social media that matches your website.

Consistency

Every touchpoint — site, socials, print — shares one visual language.

Speed

Small team, short feedback loops, no waiting weeks for updates.

Local context

We design for how Nepali audiences actually browse and buy.

Honesty

Clear pricing and timelines — no vague retainers, no surprises.
